About Samura Kitchen

Our Story

Welcome to Samura Kitchen, where we bring the authentic taste of Asian cuisine right to your doorstep. We specialize in making high-quality sushi accessible to everyone, breaking the notion that great sushi must come with a hefty price tag.

Our Chef

As a current cook at one of Maastricht's finest meat restaurants, I bring my passion for culinary excellence to every dish. My experience and dedication to quality ensure that each sushi roll is crafted with precision and care.

Our Promise

We work exclusively with fresh, market-bought ingredients to create an experience that goes beyond just a meal. Our commitment to quality and authenticity means you can enjoy restaurant-quality sushi in the comfort of your home.
